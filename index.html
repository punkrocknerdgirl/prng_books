About

Hi. I'm Ernie.

I run PRNG Bookkeeping Services out of rural Texas. I'm a QBO Level 2 and Payroll certified bookkeeper who also builds the systems and automations that make the bookkeeping actually work — and make your whole operation run smoother.

I'm Gen-X. I watched technology grow up. I wasn't born into it. That means I can translate between the analog way you've always done things and the digital infrastructure that can scale it.

I love working with clients who are in industries I've actually worked in, but I am also a quick study when it comes to researching and understanding other industries before I start to build for you.

I work best with small business owners who are done being confused by their finances, sick of doing things the hard way, and ready to hand the operational chaos to someone who genuinely loves untangling it.
